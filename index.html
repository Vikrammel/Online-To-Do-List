  • Bio

    John Smith was an english soldier, explorer, colonial governer, admiral of New England and an author. He had something to do with the creation of the first North American English colony.

    That colony was Jamestown, VA. Why am I making a website about one of the founders of Jamestown, VA? I thought I'd put 'John Smith' as a placeholder then googled it and found this dude. He mapped a bunch of new places in New England, trained people to farm so the colony didn't starve, and then had to go home to England after an "accidental gunpower explosion of gunpowder in a canoe" (???)
